Lecture 01 Introduction(Algorithm)

Oct 24, 2015 in Algorithm-MUST

General infomation

  • Course Unit: 3
  • Course Type: Compulsory for all majors
  • Instructor: Dr. Hon-Cheng Wong(黃漢青)

Objectives

  • MIIZ43 is designed to provide an introduction to the analysis and design of algorithms for graduate students.
  • The course will cover the analysis of algorithm efficiency, the force and exhaustive search, decrease-and-conquer, divide-and-conquer, transform-and-conquer, space and time trade-offs, dynamic programming, greedy technique, iterative improvement, and limitations of algorithm power.

Grading scheme

  • Written assignments: 20%
  • Midterm: 30%(Closed book)
  • Final exam: 50%(Closed book)

Text and references

  • Textbook: Levitin. Introduction to the Design and Analysis of Algorithms, Thrid Edition, Pearson, 2012
  • References

Course material

  • You may get the course material from folder “MIIZ43” on ftp://ftp.must.edu.mo/
