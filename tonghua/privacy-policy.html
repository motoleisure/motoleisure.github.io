Privacy Policy for Tonghua

Last updated: January 2025

1. Information We Collect

Tonghua is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • Voice Recordings: Audio recordings are stored locally on your device for voice cloning features
  • Stories: Generated stories are saved locally on your device
  • API Keys: User-provided API keys are stored securely in your device's keychain

2. How We Use Information

  • Voice recordings are used solely for creating personalized voice synthesis
  • Stories are saved for your convenience and offline access
  • API keys enable communication with third-party AI services (DeepSeek, MiniMax)

3. Data Storage and Security

  • All personal data is stored locally on your device
  • We do not store any personal information on our servers
  • API communications are encrypted using industry-standard protocols

4. Third-Party Services

Tonghua integrates with:

  • DeepSeek API: For story generation
  • MiniMax API: For voice cloning and speech synthesis

Please review their privacy policies for information about their data practices.

5. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Tonghua is designed for children and complies with COPPA:

  • We do not collect personal information from children under 13
  • Parental consent is required for children under 13
  • Parents can review and delete their child's information at any time

6. Your Rights

  • Delete all app data by uninstalling the application
  • Control API key usage through app settings
  • Manage voice recordings and stories within the app

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:

Email: support@tonghua-app.com

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new privacy policy on this page.

童话应用隐私政策

最后更新: 2025年1月

1. 我们收集的信息

童话应用注重隐私保护，我们仅收集提供服务所必需的最少信息：

  • 语音录音： 音频录音在您的设备上本地存储，用于语音克隆功能
  • 故事内容： 生成的故事保存在您的设备上
  • API密钥： 用户提供的API密钥安全存储在设备钥匙串中

2. 信息使用方式

  • 语音录音仅用于创建个性化语音合成
  • 故事保存以便您随时访问和离线使用
  • API密钥用于与第三方AI服务（DeepSeek、MiniMax）通信

3. 数据存储和安全

  • 所有个人数据均在您的设备上本地存储
  • 我们不在服务器上存储任何个人信息
  • API通信使用行业标准加密协议

4. 第三方服务

童话应用集成了以下服务：

  • DeepSeek API： 用于故事生成
  • MiniMax API： 用于语音克隆和语音合成

请查看这些服务的隐私政策了解其数据处理方式。

5. 儿童隐私保护（COPPA合规）

童话应用专为儿童设计，严格遵守COPPA法规：

  • 我们不收集13岁以下儿童的个人信息

6. 您的权利

  • 通过卸载应用删除所有应用数据

如果您对此隐私政策有任何疑问，请联系我们：

邮箱：support@tonghua-app.com
