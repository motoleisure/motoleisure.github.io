❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How long is the free trial?
A: You get 3 full days to try all features for free.
Q: How do I restore my purchase on a new device?
A: Open the app → Tap crown icon → "Restore Purchases" button.
Q: How do I change the app language?
A: Tap the language button in the top-right corner to switch between Chinese and English.
Q: The timer stops when I switch apps.
A: Make sure Background App Refresh is enabled for Tonova in Settings.
Q: Radio stations won't play.
A: Check your internet connection and try switching to a different station.
